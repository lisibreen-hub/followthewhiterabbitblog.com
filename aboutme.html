About Me

Hello! My name is Elizabeth Breen (or Lisi), and I am a current Library and Information Science graduate student in Boston, MA. I am concentrating in Archives Management and currently work at the Harvard Graduate School of Design. I also have an MA in Early Modern English Literature from King's College London and a BA in English Literature from College of the Holy Cross.

I have created this website for my Technology for Information Professionals class, but I hope to continue to work on it!
